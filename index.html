Who We Are

A Community Built on Compassion

Jireh Assistance Group is a community-driven nonprofit based in Point Fortin, Trinidad. We were born from a simple but powerful belief: no family in our community should go to bed hungry, without clothing, or without hope.

Through monthly food drives, community outreach, and the generosity of local donors and volunteers, we distribute food, clothing, household essentials, and encouragement to struggling families — one act of kindness at a time.

"Help us share a meal to those families in need. Your donation = someone's fresh start. Let's come together to support our brothers and sisters in need."
